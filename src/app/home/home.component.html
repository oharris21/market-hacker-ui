Market Hacker gives you up to the minute buy recommendations on stocks.

It does so by detecting trends in stock prices and determining the reliability of each trend. This app was developed with day traders in mind, as you can see by the emphasis on minute to minute price changes. Furthermore, this current version of Market Hacker works off of the day trading strategy of "dip buying". All data and buy recommendations are updated each minute. Have fun hacking the market!

HotList


The most exiting feature of this website, the HotList is where you can find a list of up to the minute buy recommendations with key data to help you plan your trade.

Screener


Here you can use six customizable filters to find stocks that fit your specific needs.

Search
